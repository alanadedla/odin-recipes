Banana Pancakes


These Banana Pancakes are sure to be a hit with the family!


Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In a second bowl, mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil and bananas.
  3. Add the dry mixture to the banana mixture and stir well.
  4. Heat a frying pan over medium high heat
  5. Scoop approximately 1/4 cut of the mixture into the pan for each pancake.
  6. Cook until pancakes are golden brown, around 3-5 minutes per side. Serve hot.
