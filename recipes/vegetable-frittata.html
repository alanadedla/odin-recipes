Vegetable Frittata


Enjoy a delicious Frittata for your a nutritious breakfast!


Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tbsp feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup broccoli, chopped
  • Chilli Flakes (to taste)

Steps

  1. Heat a small pan over medium heat
  2. In a small bowl, mix together all ingredients
  3. Pour ingredients into the heated pan, and cover with a lid
  4. Cook for 7-10 minutes

Enjoy your delicious frittata!
